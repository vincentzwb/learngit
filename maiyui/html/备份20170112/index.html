COMPANY PROFILE

Shanghai Maiyu Advertising Co.,Ltd, a professional advertising company, which supplies comprehensive quality services for high end brands including windows display, props production, event planning and execution etc, with excellent experts and practiced project implementation team in industry.

 

Adhering to persistently holding the business concept of“ INTEGRITY and RESPONSIBILITY“ , the overall team is always placing importance on supplying excellent design and services so that customer satisfaction and loyalty can be improved.

 

At present, Maiyu has branch office with prominent implementation team in Shanghai, Beijing, Guangzhou and Chengdu, which can achieve“ one-stop” service effectively and efficiently.
